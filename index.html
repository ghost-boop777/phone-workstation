      Enable one or more. Workers round-robin across enabled providers, so you can combine free quotas (e.g. 1,000 + 100 + 100 checks/mo) and go faster. Keys stay in your browser.

      More workers = faster, but stay inside free limits (most allow ~1 request/sec). Effective rate ≈ workers ÷ throttle.
